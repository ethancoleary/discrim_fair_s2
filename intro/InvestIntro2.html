You must decide how many of your tokens to invest in the following lottery.

Lottery
There is a 2/3 chance you will lose the amount you invest and a 1/3 chance you will earn two and a half times the amount you invest.
To determine whether you win or lose in the lottery, the computer will randomly pick a number: 1, 2 or 3, each with an equal probability. Thus, if you decide to invest X tokens in the lottery:

  • If the number picked is 1, your earnings from the lottery will be equal to 2.5X tokens.
  • If the number picked is 2 or 3, your earnings from the lottery will be equal to -X tokens.
Your total earnings are equal to your initial 200 tokens (your starting amount) plus your earnings from the lottery (either -X or 2.5X tokens depending on the number picked by the computer).
